Parenting

As parents, we are just doing the best we can. But how many things do you wish you knew before about your baby’s development, or the importance of the relationship you establish with your child? Here you will find bite sized versions of these big topics. There is so much to say and not every personality will adjust to a particular parenting style but we believe knowledge is the key to make those informed decisions, so here you will find useful posts that we find inspiring, thought provoking and informative. 

Please, feel free to contact us to know more.

Quick parenting tips

Observe

Take what matters to you, what you think will work for your family, and try it. Notice if any dynamics change, if there is any improvement or else. One of the main keys to being a good parent is always to observe your child.

Don't rush

It is always easier said than done, but it could be useful to remind ourselves to take some time before making decisions or doing something. Take your time to think, breath and carry on.

Be real

You are the parent of your child, not an abstract version of the ideal baby you had in mind before they were born. Be real, as reality can be surprising, astonishing, fun… but ultimately, the best way to be healthy.
