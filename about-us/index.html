About us

About the world at home

The World at Home has been created in response to the new situation that lots of families with kids are facing due to the Corona Virus Pandemic.

Its purpose is to serve as a tool for parents, carers and kids to find useful activities, information and ideas on how to make the most out of this challenging situation.

We found ourselves overwhelmed with endless lists with links about what to do with our kids and whilst some of it was great and useful, we realised that it was not always so straight forward to find activities that were simple, straight to the point and easy to do at home with stuff that you might already have. So that’s what we set up to do with The World at Home project.

We’ve been working with Early Years professionals, parents, home educators, web developers, community project facilitators and a psychologist to bring you the content. We hope you enjoy it and your family too!

 

Who are we

World at Home is collaboration between The Hug, a community interest company that supports parents in local communities and Deep Lemon, a web and app development studio.
