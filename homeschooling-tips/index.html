Homeschooling Tips

The tips on this article are very basic but hugely important if you find yourself in the new situation of having to educate your kids at home.

Thanks to Bridgeway Academy for this tips. Please, find the link here.

It’s always easier said than done and lots of this advice can seem unrealistic in your personal situation. If you like the advice but it seems a bit daunting, start changing very small things. When you see the positive changes it brings to you and your kids, you will feel encouraged to make bigger changes.
